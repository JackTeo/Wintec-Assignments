Imagine soaking in the world’s largest rooftop infinity pool and gazing down on the glittering expanse of the city from 57 levels above.

After your swim, sunbathe on a luxurious poolside lounger, or relax in the shade of a palm tree with a glass of champagne in hand.

Bites & drinks A selection of snacks and drinks (alcoholic and non-alcoholic) are available poolside from 7am – 10pm daily. Enjoy specially priced drinks from $10++ every evening from 8pm - 10pm.

Towels and sun loungers are provided for your enjoyment in the pool area. Swimwear and sun care products can be purchased at the SkyPark Gift Shop.
